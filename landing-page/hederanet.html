Four Pillars of Community Infrastructure

HederaNet transforms ordinary community members into infrastructure entrepreneurs through our integrated DePIN platform

Community Mesh Internet

Deploy plug-and-play WiFi hotspots that create self-healing mesh networks. Community members become neighborhood ISPs, earning tokens for providing internet access via Hedera microtransactions.

Impact: Internet costs drop from $50/month to $5/month

Energy Sharing Microgrids

Solar-powered households tokenize excess energy and sell to neighbors through smart contracts. Automated pricing and battery networks ensure 24/7 availability.

Impact: 80% reduction in community energy costs

AI Compute Marketplace

Local devices provide AI inference services - machine learning, text-to-speech, image processing. Ultra-low latency for African users with instant HBAR payments.

Impact: Community members earn $100-500 monthly from idle devices

Reputation & Governance

NFT-based reputation badges for reliable service providers. Hedera Consensus Service records uptime and quality. Community governance for network expansion decisions.

Impact: Transparent, community-controlled infrastructure development
